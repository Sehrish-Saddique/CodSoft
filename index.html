Dr.Abdul Sittar Edhi

The man who saved a billion lives

In recognition of his life-long unselfish work for the very poorest and for peace; for his untiring efforts finding those who need care and taking care of those that nobody else cares for.

  • Jan 1, 1928Abdul Sattar Edhi was born in January 1 , 1928 in Gujarat, British India.

  • Aug 14, 1947Edhi and his family migrated to Pakistan to a city called Karachi.

  • Sep 22, 1947Edhi found some marketing to do in a wholesale shop as a peddler.

  • Apr 18, 1951Abdul Sattar Edhi established a considerable institution which is currently called Edhi Foundation. It includes, for example, hospitals, shelters, and orphanages for vagrants where services are wide spread in Pakistan.

  • Jul 8, 2016Depressingly, Abdul Sattar Edhi was diagnosed later on with kidney failure and eventually died on 8 July 2016 at the age of 88.
